A Little Bit About Us

Your3D was founded on the sole mission of streamlining the process of bringing your project from an idea to life. Finding the right service to help you with bring your project to life is difficult and time consuming. Not only that, but 3D printing can be complex and difficult to understand for beginners. So, we set out to change it.

Simply answer a few questions about your budget, timetable, location, and your project’s specifications and goals and we take care of the rest. We provide you with a selection of the best manufacturers for you plus a basic material and process recommendation that is best suited for your project’s specs.

Once you browse your matched providers and select the best one for you, we send you and your project’s information directly to them for a quote as fast as possible.

All for free, all in seconds.
