Our Mission

Simple Service

We founded Your3D on the sole mission of streamlining the process of bringing your project from idea to reality. Finding the right service to bring your project to life is difficult, time-consuming, and costly. Not only that, but 3D printing can be complex and difficult to understand for beginners. So, we set out to bring the best service out there to you.

Answer a few questions about your budget, timetable, your project’s specifications, and we take care of the rest. For beginners, dive deeper into your project in our recommendation matrix to receive ideas for the materials and printing processes we think best for your project.

From there we give you the best providers that are matched specifically to you. Simply browse these great providers, choose the best fit, and we send you and your project’s information directly to them for a quote and manufacturing as fast as possible.

All for free, all in seconds.
